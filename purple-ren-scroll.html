Why Purple Ren

What makes us different

We combine owner-operated accountability with professional training, commercial equipment, and a systematic process that delivers consistent results — every single visit.

Family-Owned & Owner-Operated

Purple Ren is owned and operated by husband and wife. We do not send different employees from week to week — the owners are the people performing the work. This means the same familiar faces, direct communication, and personal responsibility on every visit.

AMU-Certified Cleaning Knowledge

We have completed professional cleaning training from AMU — "Materialekendskab og rengøringskemi". We understand how to select the correct cleaning products and methods for different surfaces, floor types, and challenges. Cleaning effectively is not only about effort — it's about knowledge.

Systematic Cleaning Process

Every property is cleaned according to established procedures designed to deliver reliable results and maintain high standards over time. Nothing is left to chance. Our structured approach means you receive the same quality on every visit — not just the first one.

Commercial-Grade Equipment

We use powerful commercial machine cleaners and professional cleaning equipment designed specifically for property cleaning. This allows us to effectively remove dirt, dust, debris, and particles from common areas while maintaining a consistently high standard.

Fresh Microfiber Mop System

We use 6 to 16 clean microfiber mop pads per stairwell visit, changed regularly between areas. This reduces cross-contamination and ensures dirt is removed from surfaces — never just redistributed. Each room is cleaned with a separate fresh pad.

Surface Cleaning, Not Dust Spreading

Many cleaning routines rely on traditional dusting that simply moves dirt around. At Purple Ren, we prioritize thorough surface wiping — removing dust and dirt directly from horizontal surfaces, moldings, ledges, and high-touch points rather than spreading it elsewhere.

Our Process

The Purple Ren Method

Our approach is built on consistency, quality, and attention to detail. Every property follows the same six-step routine — nothing is left to chance.

01
Property Inspection

Each visit begins with a visual walkthrough to identify areas requiring extra attention before cleaning starts. We assess the current condition and adjust our approach accordingly.

02
Professional Vacuuming

Dirt, dust, sand, and debris are removed using commercial-grade machine equipment. Every corner, edge, and landing is covered before any wet cleaning begins.

03
Surface Disinfection & Detail Cleaning

Door handles, handrails, light switches, toilet flush buttons, faucets, machine control panels, and all high-touch points are cleaned and disinfected. Moldings, trims, ledges, lamps, and cobwebs are wiped and cleared.

04
Systematic Floor Cleaning

Floors are cleaned using methods and products matched to the floor type — stone, tile, or wood. The correct product protects the surface and delivers a better result than a generic approach.

05
Fresh Microfiber Mop System

Multiple clean microfiber mop pads are used throughout the property. Pads are changed between areas to maintain hygiene and ensure dirt is removed — not redistributed across the building.

06
Final Quality Check

Every visit concludes with a quality inspection to confirm our standards have been met. If anything isn't right, we address it before leaving. Consistent quality is our promise — not an aspiration.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Who performs the cleaning?

The owners of Purple Ren personally perform the work. We do not send employees from week to week — you always get the same people, with the same standards.

What areas do you serve?

We serve housing associations, property managers, apartment buildings, and commercial properties throughout Copenhagen — including Østerbro, Nørrebro, Vesterbro, Frederiksberg, Amager, Valby, Vanløse, Brønshøj, Nordvest, and Copenhagen City Centre. If your area isn't listed, please get in touch.

Do you offer recurring service agreements?

Yes. We offer weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, and customised service plans. Each plan is tailored to your property's specific needs and schedule.

What happens if Purple Ren is on holiday or unavailable?

In the event of planned holidays or unforeseen circumstances such as illness, we work with carefully selected and trusted professional partners who share our commitment to quality. Whenever possible, customers are informed in advance of any planned absence.

What makes Purple Ren different?

We combine owner-operated service, AMU-certified professional cleaning knowledge, systematic procedures, commercial-grade equipment, a fresh microfiber cleaning system, and strict quality control. The people responsible for the business are also the people performing the work — that creates a level of accountability and care you won't get from a large cleaning company.
