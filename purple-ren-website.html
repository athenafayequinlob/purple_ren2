How We Clean

A six-step method,
every time,
no exceptions.

Every visit follows the same structured, professional process — developed over 8 years of experience and grounded in our AMU cleaning certification. Nothing is left to chance or personal interpretation.

Step 01
Property Inspection

We begin every visit with a thorough walkthrough to assess the current condition of the property and identify any areas requiring special attention before cleaning starts.

Step 02
Professional Vacuuming

Commercial-grade equipment removes all loose dirt, dust, and debris from stairwells, landings, and communal areas before any wet cleaning begins.

Step 03
Surface Disinfection

All high-touch surfaces — handrails, switches, handles, mailboxes — are systematically disinfected to eliminate bacteria and reduce cross-contamination.

Step 04
Systematic Floor Cleaning

Each floor type receives the appropriate product and technique. Stone, tile, and wood are each cleaned correctly — never damaged by a generic approach.

Step 05
Fresh Microfiber System

We use 6–16 fresh microfiber mop pads per visit, changed regularly between areas so dirt is always removed from surfaces — never just redistributed.

Step 06
Final Quality Check

Before we leave, we review the completed work against our standards. If something isn’t right, we address it immediately. Consistent quality is our promise — not an aspiration.
